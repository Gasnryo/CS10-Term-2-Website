While playing Red Dead Redemption 2, I've had plenty of interesting stories. Here are some of my favorite.

The first time I played Red Dead, I joined my friend's lobby. I spawned about 10 minutes from the town of Saint Denis, so I got on my horse to ride there. After about 10 minutes I finally arrive in the saloon that all my friends are currently in. I got off my horse and slowly started approaching the bar. The second I walked in, I watched one of my good friends, Mario collapsed due to alcohol poisoning and promptly died.



My second story is between me and a bunch of friends. When we get board while playing Red Dead, we will start a massive free for all fight between all of us, and just repetitively shoot, punch, and beat eachother up for up to an hour straight. I don't know what it is, but something about beating the absolute everything out of your best friends in a video game is somehow very fun.



One of my favorite things about Red Dead Redemption 2 is how nice the community is. In many similar online games such as GTA V, people will shoot you and be toxic. In Red Dead, meeting new players is always a great experience because almost nobody will be rude to you, and a lot of the time they will help you with whatever your current objective is. I have made quite a few friends just doing missions and talking to them after.
